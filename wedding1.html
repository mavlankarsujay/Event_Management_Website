Our Weddings Experience

Cake tastings, dress shopping, picking out colors, choosing your “first dance” song — what’s not to love about being a wedding planner? A lot of people think it’s a dream job. A lot of people are wrong. Take it from me, a seasoned planner who’s been there, done that. It’s not all fun. There’s a reason Forbes listed Event Coordinator as the number-five most stressful job in America. And that's why I got out. I know what you’re thinking: How hard can it really be? After all, aren’t weddings just overhyped parties with filet mignon, open bars, and corny DJs who play "Cupid Shuffle"? Someone wears white, someone gives a toast, someone catches a bouquet. It’s hardly rocket science. So, after three years in the business, steady clients, and features in magazines, why did I quit? To answer that question, I’ll have to start at the beginning.

Our Advantages

Expertise

We offer professional advice and expertise to ensure your day flows seamlessly, relieving you of any stress along the way and enabling you to enjoy all the special, exciting parts of planning your wedding.

Venue and Supplier sourcing

We have already built strong business relationships with reputable, reliable venues and suppliers who you can trust to deliver the level of service you would expect. Any discounts or commission offered will be passed directly on to you as the client therefore saving you money. We can assist by ensuring all suppliers are adhering to their deadlines and follow up with them accordingly.

Budget Management

We can assist with providing you with a detailed budget breakdown apportioned according to your own priorities and we will advise you so that you are able to stick within your budget. We will also be able to suggest where you can save money in particular areas, by providing alternative options for you to consider.

Professional

We are professionally trained with a recognised qualification from the QC School of Wedding and Event planning. All business conducted with our clients is dealt with in a confidential manner. We hold public liability insurance and professional indemnity insurance.
